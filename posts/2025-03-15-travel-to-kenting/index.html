Explore Kenting for Free with TPASS: Commute from Kaohsiung Main Station to Hengchun and Kenting – All Free!

2025-03-15 travel
Estimated reading time: 5 minutes

Available in 繁體中文  

To save money on commuting between Kaohsiung and Tainan recently, I purchased the Southern Taiwan (Nangaoping) TPASS. This guide provides some strategies for exploring Kenting affordably. If you’re a foreigner visiting Taiwan and plan to stay in Southern Taiwan for close to a month, purchasing the TPASS is also highly recommended.

TPASS

TPASS is like an “all-you-can-eat” monthly pass for transportation, allowing unlimited travel on various public transportation within a specific area for a fixed price. The government introduced this program to make commuting more affordable and convenient, encouraging public transport use and reducing traffic congestion.

The Nangaoping TPASS covers Tainan, Kaohsiung, and Pingtung counties, offering various monthly pass options for convenient travel within Southern Taiwan.

  • Price: 999 NTD.
  • Coverage: MRT, TRA, city buses, intercity buses, ferries, and public bicycles (first 30 minutes free) within Tainan, Kaohsiung, and Pingtung counties.
  • Purchase Method: It’s recommended to visit major stations (e.g., Zuoying Station, Kaohsiung Main Station) and inquire with station staff for purchase.

Prices vary for each county.

Exploring Kenting with TPASS

While TPASS is primarily intended for commuting, it can also be a cost-effective tool for travel. Kenting falls within the Nangaoping TPASS coverage area. Below are some travel strategies for planning your trip.

Departure and Return: Kaohsiung to Kenting

Recommended Route: 9188

  • Limited Seating: Due to its use of the Highway 88 expressway, seats are limited.
  • Fare: A one-way trip from Kenting to Kaohsiung typically starts from 300 NTD, but it’s free with the TPASS.
  • Many tourists prefer route 9188 over local buses as it doesn’t refuse short-distance rides.

Alternative Route: 9117 (Infrequent service, not recommended)

Kaohsiung Bus - Bus Information

Route 9117 operates more like a local bus, with more stops than 9188 and doesn’t use the expressway. There are multiple 9117 lines; the following clarifies which are covered by the TPASS:

TPASS Free Routes:

  • 9117: Only two runs daily.
  • 9117A: Only goes to Fangliao, not Kenting.

Routes Not Covered by TPASS:

  • 9117B: Part of the Taiwan Tourist Shuttle service.

Commuting Within Kenting

The following routes are free with the TPASS for commuting within Kenting:

  • 9188: Mentioned above.
  • 9117A: Very infrequent service.
  • Kenting Shuttle Bus

Recommended Route: Kenting Shuttle Bus

The Kenting Shuttle Bus is a series of tourist buses. Be mindful of crowds due to limited seating (though recent declines in Kenting tourism may mean fewer crowds).

Kenting Shuttle Bus Routes:
